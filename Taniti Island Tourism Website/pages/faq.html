Are there places to charge electronic devices and if so, what plug do I need?

Power outlets are 120 volts and located throughout the island in hotels, bed and breakfasts, restaurants, the public library and Internet cafes.

What are the rules regarding alcohol?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced. Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

Are there any safety issues on the island? What if I get injured during my vacation?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.


There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees to take care of anyone!

Is there a language barrier if just speak English?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

When is the best time to tour Taniti?

Anytime! However, Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

Do I need to exchange currency before my trip?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
